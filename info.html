About us

Georgian Front-end developers Awards is a platform that highlights and promotes the Georgian's top websites and honors the best designers, developers, studios, and agencies who boost the web design.

We wanted to create a platform that highlights the most fabulous websites and spots the best-talented web designers. We also wanted to build a meeting point, where digital design professionals from find inspiration, impart knowledge and experience, connect, and share constructive, respectful critiques. “Always questioning”, “always evolving”.

Why participate?

Free

Awards is all-inclusive. No fees required, from nomination to winner!

Value & credibility

Attract new visitors to your website and enhance your credibility through the marketing initiatives included in the prize package.
