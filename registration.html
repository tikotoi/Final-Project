Registration Instructions

Step 1

Prior to registration, check your "Registration Status" by logging in to your account. Review to see if there are any aspects of your current status that will prevent registration.

Step 2

Also confirm that your college, degree, and major information are current. If you see a problem, contact the Student Enrollment Services Office, your Dean's Office, or Program Director's Office for assistance.

Step 3

click on the "Registration" menu and then click on "View Holds." If you have a hold on your registration, you will receive a message at this point regarding the hold. You will need to clear the hold before you can proceed.

Step 4

For your protection, we do not give out passwords by any means other than in-person with a photo ID. Passwords may be obtained at the ITS Help Desk in Lone Mountain North, 2nd Floor, West, or by contacting the Help Desk at (415) 422-6668 or at itshelp@usfca.edu.
